## Style: notion

**Color Palette**:
- Primary: Black (#1A1A1A), dark gray (#4A4A4A)

- S1 node: Pastel blue (#A8D4F0) — completed/passed
- S2 node: Pastel yellow (#F9E79F) — current/upcoming (slightly emphasized)

- Simple text labels under each node
