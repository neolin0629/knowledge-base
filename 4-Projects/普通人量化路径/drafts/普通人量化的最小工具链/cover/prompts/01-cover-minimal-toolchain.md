# Cover Prompt — 普通人量化的最小工具链

A vertical 3:4 typographic cover image for a Chinese quantitative-investing essay. Premium dark editorial poster, flat-vector rendering, balanced mood — confident, calm, restrained, NOT loud.

## Background
- Solid dark background, near-black with a subtle cool blue undertone. Use a single deep navy color around #0B1220 to #0F1626.
- Optional very faint vertical pinstripe or 1px grid (about 3% opacity) suggesting a terminal / data canvas. Must remain almost invisible — never a busy pattern.
- A single ultra-thin horizontal hairline in muted blue (#2A3A5A, 1px) sits about one-third from the top, separating the title block from the lower visual area.

## Color rules (HARD)
- Background: deep navy / near black only.
- Accent: ONE blue family only — cobalt / electric blue. Primary accent #3B82F6, deep accent #1E40AF. No teal, no purple, no green, no orange.
- Text: cool off-white (#E6ECF5) for primary, muted slate (#8A98B5) for secondary.
- No gradients beyond a barely perceptible vignette at the corners. No glow effects, no neon bloom.

## Typography (clean sans-serif, NOT serif, NOT display)
- Use a clean modern Chinese sans-serif paired with a Latin geometric sans (think Source Han Sans / PingFang style for Chinese, Inter / Söhne style for Latin). No serifs anywhere.
- Main title (Chinese, exact text — DO NOT translate, DO NOT alter):
  「普通人量化的最小工具链」
  - Large, weight medium-to-semibold, generous letter spacing for Chinese characters, single line, horizontally centered.
  - Sits in the upper third of the canvas.
- Subtitle / pull quote (Chinese, exact text — DO NOT translate, DO NOT alter):
  「工具是平权的，判断力不是」
  - Smaller (about 35–40% of title size), regular weight, muted slate color, centered directly below the title with comfortable breathing room.
  - A tiny blue square bullet (#3B82F6, 6px) optionally precedes the line as a typographic anchor — keep it understated.
- All Chinese punctuation must be full-width and correct. No invented characters. No latin transliteration.

## Visual anchor — minimal flow icon (lower half)
- In the lower-middle of the canvas, render an ultra-minimal flow diagram suggesting "data → backtest → live trading":
  - Three small filled circles (8–12px) connected by two thin horizontal line segments.
  - Circles in cobalt blue (#3B82F6); connector lines in deep blue (#1E40AF) at 1.5px.
  - Centered horizontally; total flow width about 35% of canvas width.
  - Tiny lowercase Latin labels under each node in muted slate, in a small clean sans-serif:
    "data" — "backtest" — "live"
- This is the only graphic element. No icons of wrenches, gears, charts, candles, laptops, or human figures.

## Composition
- 3:4 vertical portrait canvas.
- Rough vertical rhythm:

  - 48–58% — Chinese subtitle.
