Ultra-minimal square quote card. Two lines of text, nothing else. Maximum white space.

LAYOUT: White background (#FFFFFF). The entire visual weight rests on two lines of centered text in the middle of the canvas. No decorative elements compete with the text.

ZONES:

- TOP ZONE (top 30%): Empty white space. No content.

- CENTER ZONE (center 40%): Two lines of quote text, vertically and horizontally centered:
  - LINE 1: "工具是平权的。" — bold, large, dark slate color (#0F172A). This line occupies about 18–20% of canvas height in font size.
  - SMALL VERTICAL GAP: About 8–10px white space between lines.
  - LINE 2: "判断力，不是。" — same bold weight and font size as line 1, steel blue color (#2563EB).
  - Below the two lines: a thin horizontal rule at about 50% canvas width, centered, color #E2E8F0, positioned ~20px below line 2.
  - Below the rule: very small attribution text "普通人量化路径 · 第 3 篇" in light gray (#94A3B8), centered.

- BOTTOM ZONE (bottom 30%): Empty white space. No content.

LABELS:
- Line 1: 工具是平权的。
- Line 2: 判断力，不是。
- Attribution: 普通人量化路径 · 第 3 篇

COLORS:
- Background: #FFFFFF
- Line 1 text: #0F172A (near black, bold)
- Line 2 text: #2563EB (steel blue, bold)
- Divider line: #E2E8F0
- Attribution: #94A3B8

STYLE: Extreme minimalism. Two lines of text with color contrast as the only visual device. White background. No icons. No borders. No gradients. No textures. No decorative shapes. The contrast between #0F172A (dark) and #2563EB (blue) carries all the visual weight. The white space around the text makes it feel calm and confident.

ASPECT: 1:1 square. Classic proportions for a standalone quote card.
