- Maintain clear visual hierarchy

## Text Style (CRITICAL)

## Style: sketch-notes

**Visual Elements**:
- Hand-drawn wobble on all lines, shapes, and borders
- Rounded cards with pastel color blocks as information sections
- Hand-drawn wave/downward arrows showing continuous loss
- Simple stick-figure character looking worried or nervous (optional, small)
- Doodle decorations: small stars, underlines
- Generous whitespace

**Typography**:
- Bold hand-drawn lettering for titles and key numbers
- ALL text in hand-drawn style — NO computer fonts
- Numbers should be very large and prominent (impact emphasis)

**Style Rules**:
- Maintain slight wobble on every line, shape, and border
- Leave color fills intentionally incomplete at edges
- No perfect geometric shapes or straight lines
