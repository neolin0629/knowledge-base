策略回测的噪声醒悟 — Atmospheric Scene

FOCAL POINT: A quant researcher at a wooden desk at night, facing a large monitor. The screen shows multiple overlapping equity curves — some going up impressively. One curve is circled in red with a question mark. A warm desk lamp casts golden light on the desk.

ATMOSPHERE: Late-night study room, warm desk lamp glow against dark room. Quiet, contemplative. Not a crisis — a moment of honest realization.

MOOD: The shift from false confidence to the beginning of real understanding. Humble but not defeated. "I thought I found alpha, but it was noise" — and that's okay.

COLOR TEMPERATURE: Warm — dominate with golden lamp light (#F6AD55, #ED8936). Room background in muted dark (#4A4A4A). Screen glow as secondary light source in cooler tones for contrast.

ENVIRONMENT DETAILS:
- Wooden desk with a notebook and pen
- Monitor showing backtest equity curves, one labeled with a small hand-drawn "?"
- A sticky note on the wall or monitor edge: 「1000 个策略 → 50 个假阳性」
- Coffee cup (half empty, warm)
- The lamp creates a pool of warm light; everything outside is softly dark

STYLE (warm):
- Rounded shapes, soft corners, no sharp edges
- Simplified stylized human figure (from behind or side profile), not photorealistic
- Hand-drawn quality touches — slightly imperfect lines
- Soft shadows without harsh edges
- Gentle gradients in the lamp light
- Cozy, personal atmosphere
- Cream (#FFFAF0) and Soft Peach (#FED7AA) accents on lit surfaces
- Terracotta (#C05621) for depth elements, Deep Brown (#744210) for grounding
- Soft Red (#E53E3E) sparingly — maybe the circled curve or the sticky note accent

Human figures: simplified stylized silhouettes or symbolic representations, not photorealistic.
Text should be large and prominent with handwritten-style fonts. Keep minimal, focus on keywords.
Clean composition with generous white space. Simple background — the desk and lamp are the focus. Wide horizontal framing: desk on left half, monitor and wall on right half.
