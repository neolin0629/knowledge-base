五关学习路径 — Atmospheric Scene / Journey

FOCAL POINT: An ascending pathway or warm-lit staircase with 5 illuminated gates/arches. Each gate represents a math discipline on the quant learning path. A small figure walks upward, having passed the first few gates.

ATMOSPHERE: Warm golden light radiates from each gate. The path feels like climbing through increasing understanding — each gate slightly taller and brighter than the last. Hand-drawn, journal-like quality. Like a personal illustrated map of a journey.

MOOD: Encouraging, honest, grounded. "This is hard, but the path exists, and you can walk it." Not corporate, not textbook — personal.

COLOR TEMPERATURE: Warm throughout. Golden light (#F6AD55,#ED8936) at each gate, Soft Peach (#FED7AA) ambient glow. Deep Brown (#744210) for path/ground. Terracotta (#C05621) for depth.

THE FIVE GATES (ascending, left to right, climbing upward):
1. 概率论 — lowest gate, warm light, small but inviting

Each gate labeled with handwritten Chinese characters. Below gate 5, a small handwritten note: 「之后，你才是 Quant」

A small simplified figure walks between gates 3 and 4 — mid-journey, not yet at the top.
