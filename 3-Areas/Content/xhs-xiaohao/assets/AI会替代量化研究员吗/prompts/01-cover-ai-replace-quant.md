## Composition

- **Title**: "AI会替代量化研究员吗？" — large, centered, clean sans-serif Chinese font, high contrast against warm background
- **Background**: Warm-toned solid or subtle gradient (amber / burnt orange / warm coral range), no noise or texture — flat and clean
- **Layout**: Title occupies ~60% of vertical space, generous whitespace top and bottom
- **Mood**: Bold, confrontational — the question mark should feel provocative, like a challenge

## Visual Elements

- No characters, no icons, no decorative illustrations — pure typography
- The question mark "？" can be slightly larger or a different weight to create visual tension
- Optional: a very thin horizontal rule above or below the title to add structure without clutter
- Color for text: dark charcoal or near-black for maximum readability on warm background

## Style Notes

- Flat-vector rendering: crisp edges, no shadows, no gradients on text
- The warmth of the background should feel inviting but not soft — this is a bold claim, not a cozy mood
- Chinese title must be the sole focal point — nothing competes for attention

## Must NOT

- No realistic humans, no silhouettes, no stock-photo style
- No English text, no subtitles, no data labels
- No gradients on the title text
- No decorative borders or frames
